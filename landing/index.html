Project Colony -- a place for collaboration and creativity

Make the world a better place, produce value and experience freedom with Project Colony

What is Project Colony

Project Colony is a blockchain freelance platfom, it enables freelancers to find and collaborate with each others.

Freelancers collaborate without paying a high fee or being stuck with ludicrous contract terms. Collaborators are encourage to produce their own human readable contracts or use open source contracts for fruitfull and humane collaboration.

Work & Collaborate on the blockchain

Membmers of Project Colony levarage blockhain technology to make international business humane and pleasant. Small to medium companies are the backbone of western countries and they deserve a to work in a place like Project Colony, which is safe, honest and focuses on human interactions.
We claim that freelancers prefer to be creative and produce value for their customers rather than reading, adjusting and writing contracts which they probably cannot enforce internationally and most likely don’t enjoy doing so. Members of Project Colony believe in frequent and honest communicaton between collaboraters to establish long lasting relationships or stop work early if collaborators don’t fit.
